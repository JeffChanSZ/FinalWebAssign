Jobseekers & Career Switches: Work-Readiness Evening Workshop

S00001

This live and interactive online workshop will guide you through the process of what to consider when you need to switch careers or industries, and how to put your best foot forward when presenting yourself to an employer in a new field after switching careers. This Includes:

  • How to get the right qualifications
  • How to identify your transferable skills
  • How to read and evaluate job advertisements and conduct industry research

Mild cognitive impairment -Online -VIC

S67890

This session provides information about mild cognitive impairment as it relates to older people, including diagnosis, progression, treatment and management strategies. You will gain knowledge in:
